Rules

Place up to 3 cheese and 3 flags on any empty squares, in any order. The Mouse runs toward cheese; flags block its path. A flag on the Mouse wins instantly. After the Mouse bites cheese, it stays for one more turn on that square, then vanishes when the Mouse leaves. If you use all 6 pieces without catching the Mouse, it escapes.
