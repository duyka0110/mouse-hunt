Rules

P1: 3 cheese (first). P2: 3 flags. Mouse runs to cheese; flags block. Flag on Mouse = win. After the Mouse eats cheese, it stays on the board for two turns: one while the Mouse is still on that square, then it disappears once the Mouse moves away. After 6 pieces, both guess one square — only a matching pick on the Mouse wins.
