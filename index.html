Rules

CheeseFlag: place up to 3 cheese and 3 flags in any order. The Mouse runs toward cheese; flags block. A flag on the Mouse wins. After a bite, cheese lingers one turn on that square, then vanishes when the Mouse leaves. If you use all 6 pieces without catching the Mouse, it escapes.

Rogue: each turn you get two random offers (Cheese, Wall, Flag, Trap, or Pass). Place on the board or Pass; the Mouse moves after each choice. Walls go on edges between tiles. Traps win when the Mouse steps on them; flags win if placed on the Mouse. Only 3 cheese and 1 trap per game—when all cheese is eaten, you lose. After 10 mouse moves, finish the current pair of choices then you lose.
